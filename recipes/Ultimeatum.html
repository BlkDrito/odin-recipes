The Ultimeatum

The Ultimeatum is a cheesburger stuffed inside a cheeseburger with two deep fried cheeseburgers as buns, and then we add our special ketchup that we get from the Himalaya's.

Ingredients

  • A cheesburger
  • Stuffed inside a cheeseburger
  • With two deep fried cheese burgers as buns
  • Then we add our special ketchup from the Himalayas
  • Only make it every 100 years

Now to make it!

  1. Step 1: Make a cheeseburger
  2. Step 2: Stuff it in a cheeseburger
  3. Step 3: Make two more cheeseburgers as buns
  4. Step 4: Finally, we add the special Himalayan ketchup
